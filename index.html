How To

1
Select
Choose a session from the home page — Adults or Kids. This opens the worship video and prayer timer.
2
Worship
Play the worship set and let the music draw you into God's presence. This is the first 70% of your session time.
3
Pray
Scroll down to the prayer points and pray through each section for the remaining 30% of your time.

The prayer sections follow the structure of the Lord's Prayer: Love God, Help Others, Our Needs, Sorry & Forgive, Stay Safe, and Thank You. Each section contains specific prayer points with prayers for both adults and kids.
