How To

1
Select
Choose a worship set from the home page — Adults or Kids. The link will open a YouTube playlist.
2
Worship
Play the set and worship for 70% of your total time. Let the music draw you into God's presence.
3
Pray
While worship plays in the background, pray the prayer points for the remaining 30% of your time.

The prayer sections follow the structure of the Lord's Prayer: Love God, Help Others, Our Needs, Sorry & Forgive, Stay Safe, and Thank You. Each section contains specific prayer points with prayers for both adults and kids.
